Welcome to Kellogg Cork & Screw

About Us

Kellogg's Community of Wine Lovers

Our Mission at Cork & Screw is to further the Kellogg student body's appreciation of, familiarity with, and accessibility to wine, to promote career related opportunities, and to host opportunities for students to socialize over wine. We enjoy bringing people together over wine, learning about wine, and best of all - drinking wine.

This year will be filled with exciting tastings and events, so bring a friend or come and make new ones!

Ever wondered how to choose wine at a business dinner? Or, how to shop the shelf at a wine store?

Join our group and check out our upcoming events!

Cheers,

Cork & Screw Exec Board

Getting Involved

Cork & Screw hosts events throughout the year that are designed to engage Kellogg wine enthusiasts at all levels of wine education. We aim to make wine inclusive and approachable for all. If you are interested in joining our Executive Board, we accept applications every October and provide a full list of available positions at our Fall Kick-Off event.

Social Events

Career & Industry

Learn about the wine industry and develop your palette by attending Cork & Screw's various wine education events, including tastings and partner events such as "How to Choose Wine for a Business Dinner."
Cork & Screw events are some of Kellogg's most fun and welcoming. We welcome all students to our happy hours throughout the year. Make sure you do not miss our annual trip to wine country each May, or the closeout event of the school year, Diner en Blanc, in June!
Recruiting for the wine industry or CPG? Cork & Screw partners with some of the world's top wine producers to facilitate networking and career development. Past sponsors include: E & J Gallo Winery, The Wine Group, and Constellation Brands.
